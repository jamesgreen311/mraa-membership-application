Membership Requirements

If you would like to become a member of Metropolitan Richmond Artists Association (MRAA), please fill out this online application. We request at least 5 examples of your work and an additional 2 that show the back of two pieces. It's important to show the framing and hanging of your work.

Exhibiting members work will be juried by the MRAA jury committee. You will receive a letter informing you of the juries decision. If you work is not accepted at this time, you will automatically become an Associate Member until you are ready to re-submit your work. You may re-submit in a subsequent quarter.

Art Work Submissions

  • In the case of 3D work show the pedestal and mounting

Image Files

  • Limit JPG file size to under 3MB each, 72dpi
  • Keep quality as high as possible to stay under the file size limit
  • Please be patient while image uploads. The larger the file size the longer it may take to upload. It may take several seconds
