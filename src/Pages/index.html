MRAA New Applicants

Exhibiting Membership: Artists wishing to join and exhibit are juried.

Associate Membership: Non-exhibiting artist membership is also encouraged.

We use an online submission platform for work to be juried for membership. We are requesting 5 examples of your work and an additional 2 that show the back of two pieces, and framing, if applicable (7 images total) . If you are unsure about how to produce online photo submissions click Photographing Art. That should answer your questions. Please be sure your work is clearly marked with your name and medium. You will soon receive a letter informing you of the decision of the jury. If your work is not accepted at this time, you will automatically become an Associate Member until you are ready to resubmit your work; you may resubmit in a subsequent quarter.

Art Work Requirements

  • Include 5 artworks with presentation (framing or pedestal/support) displayed
  • 2 images showing the back of the 2D work
  • In the case of 3D work show the pedestal and mounting

Image File Requirement

  • Limit JPG file size to under 3MB each, 72dpi
  • Keep quality as high as possible to stay under the file size limit
  • Please be patient while image uploads. The larger the file size the longer it may take to upload. It may take several seconds
